# Example: Quick Breathe

## User
"I'm so stressed right now, everything is breaking"

## Claude (using Breathe skill)

Let's pause for 30 seconds. Try the physiological sigh — it's the fastest way to calm your nervous system (Stanford research, 2023).

**Do this now:**

1. Inhale through your nose — a big breath
2. At the top, take one more small sip of air (fills your lungs completely)
3. Now exhale slowly through your mouth... let it all out
4. Again. Double inhale through nose... long exhale through mouth...
5. One more time. Double inhale... and release...

How does that feel? Shoulders dropping? Jaw unclenching?

The thing that's breaking — it'll still be there in 60 seconds. But you'll approach it with a clearer head now.
